Walkabout

A good friend and mentor of mine, Paul Taylor, lives by the idea of using stories as a way of teaching people. Walkabouts are a way of taking us through the journey of people(s) lives and helping us grasp the importance of their experiences. If you want to understand who someone is, you need to know their story. This is my professional walkabout, I hope it helps.

Student Success

The team I had the privilege to be part of during my time as a Peer Mentor Analyst. The organization was overseen by professional staff, but was managed mostly students, which was an awesome experience. It taught me a lot about what it means to be a leader and to pursue a goal out of pure passion for helping others.
